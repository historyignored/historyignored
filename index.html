Something about history has always intrigued me
Especially the History Ignored or avoided

Turns out there was plenty of history that has been ingored and avoided.
The more I learned the more I desired to know. This blog discusses the historical
information that I have learned and would like to share. After reading some of the material
on this blog, I hope you have the same passion for understanding history I do!

History is defined as the study of past events, particularly in human affairs.

"History never really says goodbye. History says, 'See you later'" -Eduardo Galeano

"Those who cannot learn from the past are doomed to repeat it" - George Santayana

Knowledge

History that has been ignored or avoided is valuable as it is often knowledge that somebody does not want you to know.

Enlightenment

The power that this hidden history provides allows you to see the world in a different light.

Foresight

History will often provide insight into the future as it allows you to see the patterns that take place throughout history.
